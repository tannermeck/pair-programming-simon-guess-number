Guessing Game

Hello! This is an app that allows you four guesses to find a number between 1 and 20 inclusive (including "1" and "20"). After each incorrect guess, I will notify you that your guess is "too high" or "too low". If you guess correct, you'll recieve a congratulatory message. If you fail to guess correctly, it's GAME OVER!
